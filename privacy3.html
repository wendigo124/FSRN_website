Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [To Be Updated Upon Launch]

This Privacy Policy describes how Sugar ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Address: Required to create an account and authenticate your access.
  • Nutritional Data: Including your food entries, dietary goals, and consumption history.

We do not collect your name, physical address, or any government-issued identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide personalized, AI-driven nutritional insights
  • Improve and support app functionality
  • Maintain your dietary history and goal tracking

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing or analysis.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party SDKs or analytics tools.

We transmit your nutrition data to OpenAI's ChatGPT API to generate dietary insights. While we do not explicitly allow them to use this data to train models, it is possible that OpenAI retains and uses API data in accordance with their policies.

We do not share your information with vendors, affiliates, or advertising networks.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: Data is stored in MongoDB and managed through a backend hosted on Heroku.
  • Retention: Your data is retained for 30 days after account deletion, after which it is permanently removed.
  • Encryption: We do not currently implement encryption at rest or in transit.

5. Your Rights

If you reside in the EU/UK, California, or other applicable jurisdictions, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion
  • Request a copy of your data

You may exercise these rights by contacting us at privacy@[pendingcompanywebsite].

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly allow users under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, contact us immediately.

7. Breach Notification

We maintain procedures to detect and notify affected users in the event of a data breach, as required by applicable law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available in the app and will list the effective date up top.
